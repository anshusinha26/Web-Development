My top 3 movies of all-time

Interstellar

"Interstellar" is a thought-provoking science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. Set in a near-future Earth on the brink of collapse, it follows a group of astronauts who journey through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. Led by Matthew McConaughey's character, they face mind-bending challenges involving time dilation and the mysteries of space as they seek to secure humanity's future. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit against the backdrop of cosmic exploration.

Avengers: Endgame

"Avengers: Endgame" is a blockbuster superhero film released in 2019, directed by Anthony and Joe Russo. As the epic conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga, it follows the Avengers as they embark on a daring mission to reverse the catastrophic events of "Avengers: Infinity War." With a star-studded cast including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, and Scarlett Johansson, the film is a thrilling culmination of over a decade of storytelling, packed with action, emotion, and surprising twists. "Avengers: Endgame" shattered box office records and left an indelible mark on pop culture as one of the most ambitious and successful superhero films ever made.
